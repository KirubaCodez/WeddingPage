How we meet in our life

She proposed, I said yes

We had been talking as friends for a while. Then one day, out of the blue, she said, “We shouldn’t talk anymore.” I didn’t fully understand what she meant — but suddenly, I found myself in tears.

Just as we were about to hang up, she softly said, “Even when you’re leaving… you still make me feel loved.”

It was June 14, 2018, at 1:45 AM — a moment etched in my heart forever. I will never forget it for the rest of my life.
